About

In the spirit of sharing a piece of my world with you, this section is a window into my journey, interests, and the experiences that shape who I am.

_____ As a Senior Software Engineer ______

I'm currently working at leading swedish based software company as a full time Senior Software Engineer with over 4+ years of IT&Engineering industry experience. I've been contributing to various projects over the last 4+ years and working with almost all the so called "03 tiers(DB, M-Tier and Client)". Recently, I've started working with DevOps technologies such as Azure administration, Kubernetes, Terraform automation and Bash scripting as well.

____ As a Freelancer,Visiting Lecturer _____

Huge fan of sharing knowledge with communities/individuals. I am doing fiver freelancing in the area of technical documentation writing, java consultation & teaching in weekends. It's my passion to help Srilankan undergraduates, interns and A/L ICT students who are keen on following a software engineering career path. So, I got an opportunity to play a new role: external lecturer of this new degree program "BSE(Bachelor of Software Engineering)" which is a collaborative effort of SLASSCOM and OUSL . I also conduct Core Java programming course for undergraduates, interns and oversee students.
